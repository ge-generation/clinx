## Respiratory, Effusion %%
Causes for a pleural exudate?
- Pneumonia
- Neoplasm
- Tuberculosis
- Sarcoidosis
- Infarction
- Pancreatitis
- Rheumatoid arthritis, Systemic lupus erythematous
- Nitrofurantoin, bromocriptine
- Radiation

## Respiratory, Effusion %%
Causes for a pleural transudate?
- Cardiac failure
- Nephrotic syndrome
- Liver failure
- Meigs' syndrome (ovarian fibroma)
- Hypothyroidism

## Respiratory, Pulmonary Fibrosis %%
Causes of lower lobe fibrosis?
- Rheumatoid arthritis
- Asbestosis
- Scleroderma
- Crytogenic fibrosing alveolitis
- Bleomycin/Nitrofurantoin/Hydralazine/Methotrexate

## Respiratory, Pulmonary Fibrosis %%
Causes of upper lobe fibrosis?
- Histiocytosis
- Ankylosing spondylitis
- Silicosis
- Tuberculosis
- Radiation
- Allergic bronchopulmonary aspergillosis
- Pneumoconiosis
- Sarcoidosis
